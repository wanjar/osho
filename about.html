Our History

Starting in 1993, Osho Chemicals initially dealt with the supply of industrial chemicals and agricultural inputs in Kenya. Since then, this dynamic and innovative company has grown tremendously by grasping various opportunities in its path.

Our team has grown from 6 employees to over 380 skilled personnel and we have expanded our regional footprint to cover Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia and Malawi.

Our Mission

To provide quality and affordable life science, industrial, farm equipment and allied products and services in East, Central and Southern Africa.

Quality policy

Osho Chemical Industries Ltd is a leader in manufacturing, distributing and marketing of Quality and Affordable Agro products, Industrial Chemicals, Public Health Products and Animal Health Products. We are committed to offering innovative and sustainable solutions to our customers in the Eastern, Central and Southern Africa. In delivering our promise “Your care is our concern, we grow with you” we commit to:


Establish and enhance partnerships with external providers and relevant interested parties to provide improved products and services.
Ensure the policy is communicated, understood, applied within Osho and is available to relevant interested parties as appropriate.
Embrace risk-based thinking, process approach and integrate QMS in our business operations.
Establish, measure, monitor and update as appropriate quality objectives that are consistent with this policy.
Conform to customer and applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
Benchmark with best global practices in the industry


This quality policy which is compatible with the purpose and context of Osho Chemical Industries Limited, supports its strategic direction, shall be reviewed to consider changing internal and external issues and other relevant practices for continual improvement.
